Arrivals

Athens International Airport - Eleftherios Venizelos ('ATH')

Athens airport is the main gateway to Athens, Greece, accessible through direct flights from major cities globally. The airport is connected to the city center (via the blue line) and the city's ports (Piraeus, Rafina and Lavrio) via public transportation, including metro and bus services, as well as taxis.

The city

Athens, Greece – A Global Stage for Innovation
A City That Shaped the World
Welcome to Athens, a city where groundbreaking ideas have shaped human progress for more than 2,500 years. Home to iconic landmarks such as the Acropolis of Athens and the Parthenon, Athens has long been a meeting point for thinkers, innovators, and global dialogue. Today, this historic capital continues to inspire new generations of leaders and visionaries.

A Dynamic Technology Gateway
As a rapidly evolving digital hub in Southeastern Europe, Greece offers an ideal environment for conversations around connectivity, mobile data, and emerging technologies. With expanding infrastructure, a thriving innovation ecosystem, and increasing international investment, Athens is positioning itself as a strategic gateway linking Europe, the Middle East, and Africa.

A Destination That Connects People
Beyond the conference sessions, Athens offers an unforgettable experience for international delegates. From vibrant cultural districts and world-class museums to exceptional Mediterranean cuisine and nearby coastal escapes, the city provides the perfect balance of business and inspiration—encouraging meaningful connections both inside and outside the conference venue.
